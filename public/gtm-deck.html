05 / Creative Strategy

Three Messages.
Built to Iterate.

Message Architecture

Layer 1 — Trust
"Your health. Covered everywhere." VUMI's 7,000-hospital network, Latin American heritage, Malta EEA clearance. This layer builds the permission to sell. It must land before anything else.
Layer 2 — Belonging
"Para quien vive entre dos mundos." Cultural resonance for the premium LATAM audience. Spain covered. Family in origin covered. One policy, two worlds. No competitor can claim this ground.
Layer 3 — Action
Visa segment: "Malta EEA compliant." Precise, regulatory, and a genuine decision-maker for the nómada digital and student segments. Cigna Global cannot compete on this specific claim.

Format by Channel

  • Broker co-brand: Long-form trust content. PDF one-pager plus 90-second video. Broker's face and name front and centre. VUMI's product in the supporting role.
  • Native content: 600–900 word health-advice article. Soft product integration from paragraph 4 onwards. Outbrain/Taboola placement.
  • Community: WhatsApp voice note from a community leader. Card graphics designed for sharing. Informal register — community voice, not brand voice.
  • Podcast: 60-second mid-roll. One product truth. Neutral Spanish — not Madrid, not LATAM. Both audiences hear themselves.
  • Onboarding: 90-second personal video. Tailored to visa type or residency status. One clear next step. No jargon.
